# Wellspring - Project Outline

## Project Name
**Wellspring**

## Project Description
Wellspring is a longevity health dashboard platform that serves as a unified source of health and vitality. It empowers health-conscious individuals to track their biomarkers, receive AI-powered personalized insights, and stay informed about the latest longevity science research.

### Brand Meaning
**Wellspring** = "a source of a desirable quality or commodity; an abundant source"
- Embodies the mission to be a continuous source of health insights and vitality
- Optimistic and nurturing tone (not clinical or alarmist)
- Modern yet warm - aligned with brand values

## Target Audience
**Primary:** Health enthusiasts aged 30-50
- Already tracking health metrics (Oura ring owners, gym-goers, supplement users)
- Familiar with biohacking and longevity concepts
- Willing to invest time and money in preventive health
- Value data-driven approaches to wellness

**Geographic Focus:** Sweden/Nordics initially, expanding globally
- Bilingual platform (Swedish + English) from launch
- Integration path with Werlabs (Swedish testing provider)
- Nordic-focused content and partnerships

## Core Use Cases

### 1. Biomarker Tracking
- **User Need:** Centralized place to track all health metrics and biomarkers
- **Solution:** Dashboard with manual entry, PDF upload, and future API integrations
- **Value:** No more scattered Excel sheets or paper reports
- **Features:**
  - Track 25+ common biomarkers (HbA1c, lipid panel, hormones, vitamins, etc.)
  - Visual trend charts showing historical data
  - Reference ranges and optimal ranges
  - Color-coded indicators for status (optimal, borderline, concerning)

### 2. AI-Powered Insights
- **User Need:** Understanding what biomarkers mean and what actions to take
- **Solution:** Claude AI-powered analysis generating personalized insights
- **Value:** Professional-grade analysis without expensive consultations
- **Features:**
  - Biomarker interpretation in plain language
  - Trend analysis detecting improvements or declines
  - Correlation insights between related biomarkers
  - Personalized protocol recommendations (premium)
  - Risk assessments (premium)

### 3. Educational Content Hub
- **User Need:** Staying informed about longevity science and best practices
- **Solution:** Curated content library with latest research and protocols
- **Value:** Trusted, accessible education without information overload
- **Features:**
  - Research summaries on key longevity topics
  - Protocol guides (Blueprint-inspired)
  - Supplement guides (Nordic-available options)
  - Latest longevity news and studies
  - Bilingual content (Swedish + English)

### 4. Goal Setting & Progress Tracking
- **User Need:** Setting health targets and monitoring progress
- **Solution:** Goal management system with achievement tracking
- **Value:** Motivation and accountability for health improvements
- **Features:**
  - Set target values for biomarkers
  - Track progress toward goals
  - Achievement celebrations
  - Quarterly health reports (premium)

## Business Model

### Freemium Model
**Free Tier:**
- Track up to 10 biomarkers
- Manual entry only
- Simple charts (6 months history)
- 5 AI insights/month (GPT-4o-mini)
- 50% of content access
- No data export

**Premium Tier ($19/mo):**
- Unlimited biomarkers
- Advanced charts & correlations
- Unlimited AI insights (Claude-powered)
- Personalized protocols
- Quarterly risk assessments
- Full content library
- Data export (CSV, PDF)
- PDF upload with extraction
- Future: Lab API integrations

### Revenue Projections
- MVP: $75/mo costs, small user base
- 1000 users (30% premium): $5,700/mo revenue, $340/mo costs = 94% margin

## Key Differentiators

### vs Neko Health
- Software-first vs hardware-dependent body scan clinics
- Affordable ($0-19/mo) vs expensive ($299 per scan)
- Ongoing tracking vs one-time assessments
- At-home convenience vs clinic visits

### vs Blueprint (Bryan Johnson)
- Accessible pricing vs $2M/year protocols
- Simplified protocols vs extreme biohacking
- Community-focused vs individual celebrity
- Practical for average person vs aspirational

### vs Werlabs
- Aggregation + insights vs just testing
- AI-powered analysis vs raw data
- Education + tracking vs one-off tests
- Cross-provider support vs single lab
